About

The inacessability of sustainable resources  has always been a problem. Especially in the last few decades, there has been increasing worry in the preservation of many things, the most prevalent being the environment. Every year, due to many factors like deforestation, 5-10% of tropical species go extinct. Even though there are many factors like overharvesting that harm the environment, the initiative that we are focusing on is the excessive use of water.  

First off, when the overall goal is to conserve water, you need to know what is the largest consumer of it. 70% of all potable water is used for agricultural practices. To make sure that this does not end up causing the depletion of all the water on earth, we are trying to put innovative horticulture and aquaponics to use.  

In the modern world, there have been numerous instances during which we have had to adapt to the environment and how there have been changes in the way that we treat the environment, and that has to change. Our society has become so used to wasting our natural resources that we have become blind to the direction that our world is heading in. With this initiative, we hope to bring our world to a better place, and make sure that we can sustain ourselves comfortably as long as possible.

HORTICULTURE 

Throughout history we struggled with Horticulture but now we are able to solve them through the use of technology and sustainable methods. One problem is the waste of water and raw materials. This issue can pose to be very difficult and could potentially become a disaster in the future if not taken care of properly. One way to solve this is to use different technologies such as soil analysis and irrigation using the quickly advancing technology of drones. This can be used in a number of ways, which makes the very expensive price card worth it for many.  

 

 

AQUAPONICS 

One huge problem in aquaponic farming is something as simple as the water tempature. The water tempature can harm and kill the fish inside of your farm. There are some temporary solutions like monitoring the air tempature and sun. The more permanant solution that we have created is a system that autonomously closes and opens blackout curtains and/or blinds whenever the heat exceeeds the set limit.
